Mental Health Screening Tools Anxiety Screening

This screening tool utilizes the GAD-7, a clinically recognized questionnaire designed to measure the severity of generalized anxiety symptoms. It reflects on your experiences over the last two weeks to help identify common emotional and physical patterns.

While these results offer valuable insight into your mental well-being, this quiz is a self-assessment tool and not a clinical diagnosis. For a formal evaluation and support, we recommend discussing your summary with a healthcare professional.
